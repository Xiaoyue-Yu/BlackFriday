# Black Friday

**A game built for the inaugural The Jam @ GDC 2026**

You are a top-tier salesperson at a clothing store during the craziest shopping day of the year: Black Friday! But your customers aren't just looking for discounts—they are looking for the perfect outfit to **celebrate** their special moments.

They won't tell you exactly what they want. You must use your excellent communication skills to navigate their vague requests, avoid conversational traps, build their trust, and recommend the clothes they *actually* need before the store closes!

##  How to Play

Customers will enter your store with a specific "celebration" in mind (e.g., an anniversary dinner, a promotion, a post-shopping party).

1. **Talk:** Greet them and listen to their initial thoughts.
2. **Persuade:** Choose your dialogue responses carefully. Finding their true hidden needs will increase their **Trust Score**. Asking the wrong questions will annoy them!
3. **Recommend:** Once you figure out what they need, head to the closet to find the matching outfit.
4. **Checkout:** Bring the clothes back to the customer and ring them up before the store hours run out.

##  Controls

* **`W` `A` `S` `D**` - Player Movement
* **`E`** - Interact (Talk to customers / Open closet / Checkout)
* **`W` / `S` + `E**` (or Left Mouse Click) - Choose dialogue response options
* **`A` / `D` + `E**` (or Left Mouse Click) - Pick clothes from the closet
* **`ESC`** - Exit closet / Open Settings & Pause

##  Core Mechanics

* **Hidden Trust Score:** Every dialogue choice matters. High trust means a successful sale; low trust means the customer might just walk away.
* **Budget Management:** Every customer has a hidden budget. (Work in progress)
* **Time is Money:** The store has opening and closing hours. A single second represents a minute of in-game time. Serve as many customers as you can before the timer runs out!
